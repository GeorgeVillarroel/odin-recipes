Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • 200g of ground beef
  • 1/2 of Onion, 1/4 of a garlic
  • 1 can of tomato pure
  • 3-4 lasagna pasta sheets
  • Sugar, salt, paprika, basil parsley, black pepper
  • Mozzarella and parmesan cheese

Recipe steps

  1. Saute the onion and garlic for 4 mins in a saucepan, add the spices of choice, and mix for a few mins until spices are fragant
  2. Add the meat and cook for 5 mins while stirring, then add the tomato pure, with the fire on max boil the sauce, once boiling lower the heat and let it simmer for 10 minutes
  3. Cook the pasta on boiling water with salt, once the pasta is done per packet instructions put it aside
  4. Now for the last step to layer the lasagna you have to put pasta sheet, meat, sauce, cheese, pasta sheet, meaty sauce, cheese, then you put it on the oven for the cheese to gratinate and its ready to serve
